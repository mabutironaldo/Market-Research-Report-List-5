<p><p>Continuous vacuum pans are commonly found in four main types in the market: Dual Continuous, Split Continuous, Vertical, and Horizontal. Dual Continuous pans consist of two separate bodies, allowing for continuous processing without interruptions. Split Continuous pans have a separate vessel for crystallization, making them ideal for high-capacity operations. Vertical vacuum pans have a vertical orientation for enhanced efficiency, while Horizontal vacuum pans have a horizontal layout, suitable for compact spaces and smaller scale production. Each type caters to specific needs and preferences in the industry.</p></p>
