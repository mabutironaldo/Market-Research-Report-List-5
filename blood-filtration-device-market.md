<p><p>Blood filtration devices are crucial in medical procedures such as hemodialysis, leukocyte filtration, and plasma separation. Hemodialysis membranes are used to filter waste and excess fluids from the blood in patients with kidney failure. Leukocyte filters are employed to remove white blood cells during blood transfusions to prevent adverse immune reactions. Plasma separation membranes separate plasma from blood cells for various medical applications. Each type of blood filtration device serves a specific purpose in maintaining or improving a patient's health.</p></p>
