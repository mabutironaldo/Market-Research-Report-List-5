<p><p>Bone Densitometry Equipment market consists of two main types: Ultrasound Bone Densitometer and X-ray Bone Densitometer. Ultrasound Bone Densitometers use sound waves to measure bone density, making them portable and affordable options for screening purposes. On the other hand, X-ray Bone Densitometers utilize X-rays to provide more accurate and detailed measurements of bone density, making them ideal for diagnosing osteoporosis and monitoring treatment progress. Both types play a crucial role in assessing bone health and preventing fractures.</p></p>
