<p><ul><li>IMM</li><li>Extrusion Machines</li><li>BMM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plastic processing machinery market is segmented into Injection Molding Machines (IMM), Extrusion Machines, and Blow Molding Machines (BMM). IMM are used for producing plastic components by injecting molten material into a mold. Extrusion machines create plastic profiles by pushing material through a die. BMM are used to create hollow plastic products by blowing air into molten material. These machinery types cater to different production needs in the plastic industry, offering versatility and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
