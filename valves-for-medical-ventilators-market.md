<p><p>Valves for medical ventilators come in two types: single-use and reusable. Single-use valves are designed for one-time use only and are disposed of after each patient. They help prevent cross-contamination and ensure sterility. Reusable valves are designed to be cleaned and sterilized between uses, making them more cost-effective in the long run. Both types play a crucial role in controlling the flow of gases in medical ventilators, ensuring the proper delivery of oxygen and other gases to patients in need of respiratory support.</p></p>
