<p><p>Trim systems play a crucial role in optimizing the performance and handling of boats and other marine vessels. There are two main types of trim systems in the market - trim tabs and interceptors. Trim tabs are adjustable surfaces located on the trailing edge of a boat's hull, which help in adjusting the vessel's pitch and roll. On the other hand, interceptors are retractable blades mounted on the transom of the boat, which provide dynamic lift and help in improving overall stability and efficiency of the vessel.</p></p>
