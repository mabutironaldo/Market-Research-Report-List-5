<p><p>Periodontal probes come in two main types - single-ended and double-ended. Single-ended probes have a sharp point at one end for probing pockets and measuring pocket depth. Double-ended probes have a sharp point at one end and a ball or blunt tip at the other end for measuring furcation involvement and assessing tooth mobility. Each type of probe is used for different purposes in periodontal examination and treatment, providing dentists with the necessary tools to accurately diagnose and monitor periodontal health.</p></p>
