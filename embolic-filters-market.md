<p><strong>The Embolic Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upper Chamber Embolic Filter</li><li>Inferior Chamber Embolic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embolic filters are devices used in medical procedures to capture blood clots or other debris that may break loose during the procedure and cause blockages in blood vessels. Upper chamber embolic filters are placed in the arteries leading to the brain, preventing clots from reaching this vital organ. Inferior chamber embolic filters are placed in the veins leading to the heart or lungs, preventing clots from traveling to these critical areas. Both types of embolic filters help to reduce the risk of complications and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
