<p><p>Platform Screen Doors (PSD) are physical barriers installed on train platforms for passenger safety. Full-Closed Type PSD completely encloses the platform edge, while Semi-Closed Type PSD partially encloses it. Half-Height Type PSD only covers the lower half of the platform edge. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences of transport systems. Full-Closed Type offers maximum protection and security, Semi-Closed Type balances safety with accessibility, and Half-Height Type provides basic protection while allowing for more open communication between passengers and staff.</p></p>
