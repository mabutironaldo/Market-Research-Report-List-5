<p><p>Wire mesh decks are commonly used in pallet racking systems to provide stability and safety for storing items. The different types of wire mesh decks include Standard U-Channel Decks, Inverted Type Decks, Flush Flat Decks, Inside Waterfall Decks, Flared Type Decks, and Others. Standard U-Channel Decks have a U-shaped channel for support, while Inverted Type Decks have the wire mesh on the bottom. Flush Flat Decks have a flat surface for even distribution of weight, while Inside Waterfall Decks have a lip that fits inside the beam. Flared Type Decks have angled sides for added support and stability.</p></p>
