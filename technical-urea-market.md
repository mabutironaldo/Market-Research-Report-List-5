Small particles are commonly used in applications requiring a faster release of nitrogen, such as foliar sprays. Medium particles are versatile and commonly used in agriculture for soil applications. Large particles are used for slow-release formulations and are ideal for long-term fertilizer programs. Each market type caters to specific needs and applications within the industry.</p></p>
