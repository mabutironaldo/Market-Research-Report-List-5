<p><p>Compact washers come in two main types - top load and front load. Top load washers have a hinged lid on the top of the machine, allowing users to easily add clothes even mid-cycle. They are typically more affordable and have a faster washing cycle. Front load washers, on the other hand, have a door on the front, making it easier to load and unload laundry. They are known for their efficiency, using less water and energy compared to top load washers.</p></p>
