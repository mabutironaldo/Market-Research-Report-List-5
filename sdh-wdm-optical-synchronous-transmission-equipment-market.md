<p><p>SDH WDM optical synchronous transmission equipment market includes both SDH (Synchronous Digital Hierarchy) and WDM (Wavelength Division Multiplexing) technologies. SDH is a standard for synchronous data transmission over optical fibers, providing high-speed communication for voice and data traffic. WDM, on the other hand, allows multiple signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single optical fiber using different wavelengths of light. Both SDH and WDM technologies play a crucial role in the expansion and optimization of optical communication networks.</p></p>
