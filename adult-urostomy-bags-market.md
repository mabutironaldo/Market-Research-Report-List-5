<p><p>Adult urostomy bags are categorized based on the type of flange they have: flat flanges and convex flanges. Flat flanges have a flat surface that adheres to the skin around the stoma, providing a secure and comfortable fit. Convex flanges, on the other hand, have a raised, curved surface that helps improve adherence and prevent leaks for individuals with uneven or retracted stomas. Both types cater to different needs and preferences of individuals requiring urostomy care.</p></p>
