<p><p>Intumescent acrylic sealants are available in two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based sealants are popular for their low VOC content, making them environmentally friendly and safe for indoor use. They are easy to clean up and typically have a faster curing time. Solvent-based sealants are known for their durability and resistance to moisture and temperature fluctuations. They are more commonly used in outdoor applications where added strength and resilience are required. Both types offer fire resistance and are commonly used in construction and building projects.</p></p>
