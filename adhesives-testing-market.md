Peel strength testing measures the force required to peel apart two bonded materials. Tack strength testing evaluates the adhesive's ability to stick quickly upon contact. Shear strength testing assesses the adhesive's ability to withstand forces applied parallel to the bonded surfaces. These tests help determine the durability and reliability of adhesives in various applications, ensuring they meet industry standards and requirements.</p></p>
