<p><strong>The Curved Escalator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isokinetic Operation</li><li>Frequency Conversion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Curved escalators can be operated in two main ways - Isokinetic Operation and Frequency Conversion. In Isokinetic Operation, the escalator maintains a constant speed regardless of the load, providing consistent performance. On the other hand, Frequency Conversion allows the speed of the escalator to be adjusted based on the traffic flow, offering more flexibility and energy efficiency. Both market types cater to different customer needs, with Isokinetic Operation focusing on reliability and Frequency Conversion emphasizing adaptability.</p></p>
