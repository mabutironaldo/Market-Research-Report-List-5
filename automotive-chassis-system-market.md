<p><p>The automotive chassis system market is divided into various types such as front axles, rear axles, corner modules, and active kinematics control. Front axles are responsible for steering and supporting the front wheels, while rear axles support the rear wheels. Corner modules include components like suspension, brakes, and steering systems. Active kinematics control involves technologies that optimize vehicle handling and stability by adjusting suspension and steering systems in real-time. These different market types play crucial roles in ensuring the performance and safety of vehicles.</p></p>
