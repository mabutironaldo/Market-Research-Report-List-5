<p><ul><li>Stationary Magnetometers</li><li>Portable Magnetometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stationary magnetometers are typically installed in fixed locations such as harbors, ports, and docks to continuously monitor magnetic anomalies in the water. They provide real-time data on the Earth's magnetic field, which is crucial for navigation and safety in marine environments. On the other hand, portable magnetometers are handheld devices that can be easily carried onboard ships or boats for on-the-go magnetic field measurements. They are compact, lightweight, and ideal for conducting surveys or inspections in various marine settings.</p></p>
