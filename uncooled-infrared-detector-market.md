<p><ul><li>Bolometer</li><li>Pyroelectric Detector</li><li>Thermopile Detector</li><li>Diode Detector</li><li>Thermal Capacitance Detector</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled infrared detector market consists of several types of detectors including bolometer, pyroelectric detector, thermopile detector, diode detector, thermal capacitance detector, and others. Bolometers work by measuring the change in resistance of a material when exposed to infrared radiation. Pyroelectric detectors generate an electric signal in response to changes in temperature. Thermopile detectors use thermocouples to measure infrared radiation. Diode detectors utilize the photovoltaic effect to convert infrared radiation into an electric signal. Thermal capacitance detectors measure changes in heat capacity. Other types of detectors in this market include microbolometers, pyroelectric arrays, and quantum well infrared photodetectors.</p></p>
