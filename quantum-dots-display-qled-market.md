<p><p>Quantum Dots Display (QLED) technology is mainly divided into two market types: Quantum Dot Enhancement Film (QDEF) and direct Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diode (QLED). QDEF is a film that enhances the color and brightness of traditional LCD displays. On the other hand, direct QLED technology eliminates the need for LCD panels by directly emitting light using quantum dots. Both types offer improved color accuracy, brightness, and energy efficiency compared to traditional display technologies, making them popular choices in the display market.</p></p>
