<p><p>Modified polypropylene material market is comprised of two major types: physical modification and chemical modification. Physical modification involves altering the physical properties of polypropylene through processes like blending, compounding, or melt extrusion. This type of modification aims to improve characteristics like strength, flexibility, or impact resistance. On the other hand, chemical modification involves introducing chemical additives or altering the molecular structure of polypropylene to enhance properties such as flame retardancy, UV resistance, or thermal stability. Both types of modifications cater to specific market needs and applications.</p></p>
