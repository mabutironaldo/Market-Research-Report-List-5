Acrylic resins provide improved gloss and scratch resistance. Polyamide resins offer excellent chemical and water resistance. Polyurethane resins are known for their flexibility and durability. Each type of resin serves specific purposes in ink formulations, meeting various performance requirements in the printing industry.</p></p>
