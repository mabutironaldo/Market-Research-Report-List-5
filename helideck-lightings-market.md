<p><p>Helideck lightings are essential for safe helicopter operations on offshore platforms. Circle-H lights provide a visual landing target for pilots, while floodlights offer general illumination. Perimeter lights outline the helideck, guiding aircraft during approach and departure. Status lights indicate the operational status of the helideck. Windsocks help pilots gauge wind direction and speed. Obstruction lights warn of any obstacles near the helideck. Each type of lighting serves a specific purpose in ensuring the safety and efficiency of helicopter operations on offshore platforms.</p></p>
