<p><p>The Mid-Infrared Ultrafast Fiber Laser market consists of two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse. Continuous Wave lasers emit a constant output of energy over time, making them ideal for applications requiring a steady beam. Pulse lasers, on the other hand, emit short bursts of energy at rapid intervals, making them suitable for precision cutting and drilling. Both CW and Pulse lasers have their own unique advantages and are utilized in various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
