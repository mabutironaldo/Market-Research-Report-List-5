<p><p>Endotoxin testing systems are used to detect bacterial endotoxins in pharmaceutical and medical products. There are various types of endotoxin testing systems available, including turbidimetric, chromogenic, rFC assays, gel clot, and others. Turbidimetric systems measure the formation of turbidity in the presence of endotoxins, while chromogenic systems rely on a color change reaction. rFC assays use a recombinant protein to detect endotoxins, and gel clot systems involve clot formation in the presence of endotoxins. Other systems may use alternative methods for endotoxin detection.</p></p>
