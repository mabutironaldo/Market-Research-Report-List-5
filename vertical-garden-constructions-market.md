<p><p>Vertical Garden Constructions market can be classified into four main types: Loose Media, Mat Media, Sheet Media, and Structural Media. Loose Media systems involve planting directly into a loose growing medium. Mat Media uses pre-planted mats that can be easily installed onto vertical surfaces. Sheet Media utilizes sheets of material with pockets for plants to grow in. Structural Media involves using specially designed panels or containers that support and nourish the plants. Each type offers unique benefits and design options for creating vertical gardens.</p></p>
