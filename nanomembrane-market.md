<p><p>Nanomembrane market is categorized into three types based on the material composition: Polymeric membrane, Hybrid membrane, and Inorganic membrane. Polymeric membrane is made from various polymers, offering flexibility and high selectivity. Hybrid membrane combines properties of both organic and inorganic materials, providing enhanced performance. Inorganic membrane is composed of ceramic or metallic materials, offering superior chemical and thermal stability. Each type of nanomembrane has its unique benefits and applications in various industries such as water treatment, healthcare, and electronics.</p></p>
