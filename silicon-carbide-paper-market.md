<p><p>Silicon Carbide Paper is available in two main types: Self-adhesive and Non-adhesive. Self-adhesive Silicon Carbide Paper comes with a sticky backing that allows for easy attachment to sanding blocks or machines. This type is convenient for quick and precise sanding applications. On the other hand, Non-adhesive Silicon Carbide Paper does not have a sticky backing and requires the use of adhesives or clamps during sanding. This type offers more flexibility in terms of application methods and is suitable for a wider range of surfaces.</p></p>
