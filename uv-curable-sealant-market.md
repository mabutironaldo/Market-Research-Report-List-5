<p><p>UV curable sealants are categorized into two main types based on their flexibility: rigid sealants and flexible sealants. Rigid sealants are formulated to provide strong and durable bonds, making them ideal for applications where hardness and structural integrity are crucial. On the other hand, flexible sealants are designed to maintain elasticity and accommodate movement, making them suitable for sealing joints and gaps in materials that expand or contract. Both types of UV curable sealants offer high performance and reliable adhesion properties for various industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
