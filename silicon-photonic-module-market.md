<p><p>The Silicon Photonic Module market consists of various types of products including Transceivers, Variable Optical Attenuators, Switches, Cables, and Sensors. Transceivers are used for transmitting and receiving optical signals in communication systems. Variable Optical Attenuators are used to adjust the power level of optical signals. Switches are used to selectively route optical signals. Cables are used for connecting various components in a photonic system. Sensors use photonic technology to measure and detect various physical or chemical parameters.</p></p>
