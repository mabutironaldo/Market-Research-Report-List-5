<p><strong>The Commercial Aircraft Tire Pressure Monitoring System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Aircraft Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market consists of two main types - Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses pressure sensors mounted on each tire to provide real-time tire pressure data to the aircraft's cockpit. On the other hand, Indirect TPMS utilizes the aircraft's existing ABS system to monitor tire pressure by measuring the rotational speed of each tire. Both systems help ensure proper tire inflation, reduce the risk of tire blowouts, and enhance overall safety during flight.</p></p>
