<p><p>The gas film market includes two main types: Porous Membrane and Homogeneous Membrane. Porous Membrane is composed of a porous material that allows gas molecules to pass through, while blocking other substances. This type of membrane is widely used in gas separations and purifications. On the other hand, Homogeneous Membrane is made of a single phase material that provides a uniform barrier for gas molecules. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as healthcare, food and beverage, and environmental protection.</p></p>
