<p><p>Industrial roller chains are commonly categorized into two types based on their strand configuration: single strand and multiple strand chains. Single strand chains consist of a single row of links, while multiple strand chains have two or more rows of links running parallel to each other. Single strand chains are typically used in simpler applications, while multiple strand chains are preferred in heavy-duty industrial settings where higher strength and load capacity are required. Both types of chains offer reliable and efficient power transmission in various industrial applications.</p></p>
