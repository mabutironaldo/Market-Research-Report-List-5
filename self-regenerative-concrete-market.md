<p><strong>The Self Regenerative Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capsule Self Regenerative Concrete</li><li>Intrinsic Self Regenerative Concrete</li><li>Vascular Self Regenerative Concrete</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self regenerative concrete is a cutting-edge material that can repair itself when cracks occur, extending its lifespan and reducing maintenance costs. There are three main types of self regenerative concrete: Capsule self regenerative concrete contains capsules of healing agents that are released when cracks form. Intrinsic self regenerative concrete has the healing agents integrated into the material itself. Vascular self regenerative concrete uses a network of tubes to deliver the healing agents to the damaged areas. These different types offer unique advantages for various applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
