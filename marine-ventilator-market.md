<p><p>The Marine Ventilator Market includes various types such as Axial Flow, Centrifugal, and others. Axial flow ventilators function by moving air parallel to the shaft, making them ideal for applications requiring high airflow at low pressure. Centrifugal ventilators, on the other hand, use rotating impellers to increase the velocity of air. Other types of marine ventilators may include mixed flow or cross flow designs. Each type of ventilator offers unique benefits and is used in different marine applications based on their specific performance characteristics.</p></p>
