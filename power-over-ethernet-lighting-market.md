<p><strong>The Power Over Ethernet Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and Ics</li><li>Powered Device Controllers and Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) lighting market consists of two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers and ICs, and Powered Device (PD) Controllers and ICs. PSE controllers and ICs are components that manage the power delivery from the network switch to the lighting fixtures. On the other hand, PD controllers and ICs are responsible for controlling the power consumption and communication between the lighting fixture and the network switch. Both types play a crucial role in the efficient functioning of PoE lighting systems.</p></p>
