<p><strong>The Vehicle Emission Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers</li><li>Flame Ionization Detector (FID)</li><li>Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA)</li><li>Constant Volume Sampler (CVS)</li><li>Other Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vehicle emission analyzers can be categorized into different types based on their detection technology. Non-dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Analyzers measure gases by their absorption of infrared radiation. Flame Ionization Detector (FID) analyzes hydrocarbons by their ionization in a flame. Chemiluminescence Analyzer (CLA) detects pollutants through their light emission during chemical reactions. Constant Volume Sampler (CVS) collects and analyzes exhaust gases at a constant volume. Other types of analyzers may include portable emissions measurement systems (PEMS) or Fourier-transform infrared (FTIR) analyzers.</p></p>
