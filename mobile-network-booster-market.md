<p><p>An analog signal booster is designed to enhance the strength of analog signals, such as those used in older cell phones or non-smart devices. On the other hand, a digital signal booster is meant for boosting the strength of digital signals, like those used in modern smartphones. Both types of boosters work by capturing weak signals from the surrounding area and amplifying them to improve call quality, data speeds, and overall connectivity. Digital signal boosters are becoming more popular due to the widespread use of digital technology in today's mobile devices.</p></p>
