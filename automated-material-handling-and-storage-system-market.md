<p><p>Automated Material Handling and Storage System market includes various types such as Automated Storage and Retrieval System (AS/RS), Automatic Guided Vehicle (AGV), Conveyor & Sorter Systems, and Robotic Systems. AS/RS involve computer-controlled systems that automatically place and retrieve loads from specific storage locations. AGV are vehicles that are guided along defined pathways to transport materials within a facility. Conveyor & Sorter Systems automate the process of moving products along a conveyor belt and sorting them into designated locations. Robotic Systems utilize robots to handle tasks such as picking, packing, and palletizing goods.</p></p>
