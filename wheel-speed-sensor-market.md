The Hall Wheel Speed Sensor utilizes Hall effect technology to detect changes in magnetic fields, while the Magnetic Electric Wheel Speed Sensor uses magnets and electric currents to determine wheel speed. Both sensors are essential for ensuring accurate data for vehicle stability control systems and anti-lock braking systems.
