<p><p>Portable Filtration System Market consists of three main types: Pressure Filtration, Centrifugal Filtration, and Vacuum Filtration. Pressure Filtration systems use pressure to filter liquids through a filter medium, while Centrifugal Filtration systems rely on centrifugal force to separate solid particles from liquids. Vacuum Filtration systems create a vacuum to draw liquid through a filter. Each type has its own unique benefits and applications, providing portable filtration solutions for various industries and purposes.</p></p>
