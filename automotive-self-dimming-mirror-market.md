<p><strong>The Automotive Self-dimming Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Outer Self-dimming Mirror</li><li>Inside Self-dimming Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive self-dimming mirror market consists of two main types: outer self-dimming mirrors and inside self-dimming mirrors. Outer self-dimming mirrors are located on the exterior of the vehicle and automatically dim to reduce glare from headlights behind the car. Inside self-dimming mirrors are mounted inside the vehicle and function the same way as outer mirrors to improve driver visibility and safety. Both types of self-dimming mirrors use sensors to detect and adjust to changing light conditions.</p></p>
