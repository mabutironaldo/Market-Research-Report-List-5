<p><p>Travel sickness drugs market is segmented into three main types: Anticholinergic drugs, Antihistamines, and Others. Anticholinergic drugs work by blocking certain neurotransmitters in the brain to alleviate symptoms of motion sickness. Antihistamines reduce the effects of histamine, a substance in the body that triggers symptoms of motion sickness. Other types of travel sickness drugs may include combination medications or natural remedies. Each type of drug works in a different way to help prevent or relieve symptoms of motion sickness during travel.</p></p>
