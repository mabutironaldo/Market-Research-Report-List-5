<p><p>Portable sulfur analyzers are available in three main types: tubular sulfur analyzer, electric arc sulfur analyzer, and high-frequency sulfur analyzer. Tubular sulfur analyzers use a heated tube to combust samples and detect sulfur. Electric arc sulfur analyzers use an electric current to combust samples and measure sulfur content. High-frequency sulfur analyzers utilize a high-frequency induction furnace for sample combustion. Each type of portable sulfur analyzer offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications based on specific analytical needs.</p></p>
