<p><p>Rapid food safety test kits are divided into two main types: immunoassay and hybrid tests. Immunoassay tests detect food contaminants by measuring the interaction between antigens and antibodies. This method is commonly used for allergen testing. Hybrid tests combine different testing methods, such as DNA and immunoassay techniques, to provide more accurate and reliable results. Both types of test kits are essential for ensuring the safety and quality of food products, especially in fast-paced environments like restaurants and food processing plants.</p></p>
