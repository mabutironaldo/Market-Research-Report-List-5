<p><strong>The Titanium Forging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impression Die</li><li>Open Die</li><li>Rolled Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Titanium forging market can be segmented into three types: Impression Die, Open Die, and Rolled Ring. Impression Die forging involves compressing a workpiece between two dies, creating complex shapes with high precision. Open Die forging uses flat dies to shape the workpiece through repeated hammering or pressing. Rolled Ring forging involves shaping a metal bar into a ring shape using specialized machinery. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of complexity, size, and cost-effectiveness for producing high-quality titanium components.</p></p>
