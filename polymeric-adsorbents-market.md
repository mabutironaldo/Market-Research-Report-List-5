<p><p>Polymeric adsorbents are classified into different types based on their chemical composition and structure. Aromatic adsorbents are made from aromatic compounds and have high surface area for adsorption. Modified aromatic adsorbents have additional functional groups to enhance adsorption capabilities. Methacrylic adsorbents are made from methacrylic acid and have good mechanical strength. Other types of polymeric adsorbents include those made from different polymers or copolymers. Each type offers unique properties and advantages for various adsorption applications.</p></p>
