<p><p>Hypromellose acetate succinate polymers are categorized into three different grades - L Grade, M Grade, and H Grade based on their molecular weight and properties. L Grade polymers have lower molecular weight and are suitable for applications requiring quick disintegration. M Grade polymers have medium molecular weight and are commonly used in sustained-release formulations. H Grade polymers have higher molecular weight and are preferred for extended-release formulations. Each grade offers unique properties and is selected based on the specific requirements of the formulation.</p></p>
