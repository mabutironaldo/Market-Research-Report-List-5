<p><p>Faceshield screens come in three different lengths: 3/4 length, full length, and half length. 3/4 length faceshields cover most of the face while leaving the chin area slightly exposed. Full length faceshields provide complete coverage from the top of the head to the chin. Half length faceshields cover only the upper portion of the face, leaving the chin and lower face exposed. Each type of faceshield screen offers varying levels of protection, with full length providing the most coverage and half length offering the least.</p></p>
