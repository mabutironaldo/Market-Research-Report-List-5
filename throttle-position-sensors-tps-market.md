<p><p>Throttle Position Sensors (TPS) are used in vehicles to monitor the position of the throttle. There are two main types of TPS in the market: End Switches Type and Potentiometer Type. The End Switches Type TPS are equipped with mechanical end switches that indicate when the throttle reaches its maximum or minimum position. On the other hand, Potentiometer Type TPS use a potentiometer to provide a smooth and continuous signal indicating the throttle position. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient engine performance.</p></p>
