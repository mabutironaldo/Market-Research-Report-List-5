<p><p>Towline conveyors can be classified into two main types: push-button control and Towveyor drive. Push-button control systems allow operators to start, stop, and control the speed of the conveyor using a simple push-button interface. On the other hand, Towveyor drive systems use a dedicated motor to power the conveyor along the track. Both types offer efficient and controlled movement of materials along the conveyor line, with push-button control being more manual and Towveyor drive being more automated.</p></p>
