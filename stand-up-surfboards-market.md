<p><p>Stand-up surfboards are usually divided into two market types: soft-top and hard-top. Soft-top surfboards are made of soft foam material, making them ideal for beginners and those looking for a forgiving board. They are also less expensive and more lightweight. On the other hand, hard-top surfboards are made of tougher materials like fiberglass or epoxy, providing better performance and durability. They are favored by more advanced surfers who want more responsiveness and control on the waves. Each market type caters to different skill levels and preferences in the surfing community.</p></p>
