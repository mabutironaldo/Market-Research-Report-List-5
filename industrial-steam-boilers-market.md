<p><p>Industrial steam boilers are classified based on their design and construction. Fire tube or shell boilers consist of a jacketed vessel in which hot gases from combustion pass through tubes immersed in water. Water tube boilers, on the other hand, have water flowing through tubes while hot gases surround them. There are also other types of boilers in the market that are specifically designed for certain industries or applications, such as electric boilers or biomass boilers.</p></p>
