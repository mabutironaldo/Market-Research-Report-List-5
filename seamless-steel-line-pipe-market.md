<p><p>Seamless Steel Line Pipe market is segmented into three main types: LSAW Pipes, SSAW Pipes, and Other Pipes. LSAW (Longitudinal Submerged Arc Welding) Pipes are manufactured by bending and welding steel plates. SSAW (Spiral Submerged Arc Welding) Pipes are made by forming a spiral shape with a steel strip and welding the edges. Other Pipes include ERW (Electric Resistance Welding) and seamless pipes. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various applications within the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
