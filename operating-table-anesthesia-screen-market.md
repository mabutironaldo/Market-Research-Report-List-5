<p><p>Operating table anesthesia screens come in three main types - L-shaped, U-shaped, and others. L-shaped screens are designed with a straight edge that connects to the operating table, providing a barrier between the surgical field and the anesthesia equipment. U-shaped screens have a curved edge that wraps around the patient's head, offering more comprehensive protection. The "Others" category includes screens with unique shapes and features that cater to specific surgical needs. Each type of screen serves the purpose of minimizing contamination and maintaining a sterile environment during surgery.</p></p>
