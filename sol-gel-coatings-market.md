<p><p>Sol-Gel Coatings Market is divided into two main types: Resin coatings and Inorganic coatings. Resin coatings are formed by the hydrolysis and condensation of organic precursors, resulting in a flexible and durable coating with good adhesion properties. On the other hand, Inorganic coatings are formed by the hydrolysis and condensation of inorganic precursors, resulting in a hard and abrasion-resistant coating with excellent thermal, chemical, and UV resistance. Both types of coatings offer a wide range of applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, electronics, and construction.</p></p>
