<p><p>Authentication systems market can be categorized into three types: Single Factor Authentication, Two Factor Authentication, and Multi Factor Authentication. Single Factor Authentication relies on a single form of verification, such as a password or PIN. Two Factor Authentication requires two methods of verification, usually a password and a code sent to a mobile device. Multi Factor Authentication goes a step further by incorporating multiple forms of verification, such as biometrics or security tokens, to enhance security and protect against unauthorized access.</p></p>
