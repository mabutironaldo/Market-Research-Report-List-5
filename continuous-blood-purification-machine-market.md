<p><p>Continuous blood purification machines are used for renal replacement therapy in patients with acute or chronic kidney failure. There are two main types of these machines: mobile and fixed. Mobile machines are portable and can be easily moved from one location to another, making them ideal for use in hospitals, clinics, and even in home settings. Fixed machines are larger and more permanently installed in healthcare facilities, providing a more stable and consistent option for continuous blood purification therapy. Both types of machines play a crucial role in managing patients with kidney failure.</p></p>
