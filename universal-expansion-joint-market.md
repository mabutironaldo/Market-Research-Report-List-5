<p><p>Universal expansion joints are available in two main types: pipe end type and flange type. Pipe end type expansion joints are designed to be welded directly onto the piping system, while flange type expansion joints are connected using flanges. Pipe end type joints are typically used in applications where space is limited, while flange type joints are commonly used in applications where easy installation and removal are necessary. Both types of expansion joints provide flexibility, vibration absorption, and thermal expansion relief in piping systems.</p></p>
