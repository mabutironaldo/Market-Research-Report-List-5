<p><strong>The Quartz Countertops Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press Molding</li><li>Casting Molding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz Countertops Market includes two main types - Press Molding and Casting Molding. Press Molding involves compressing crushed quartz and resin under high pressure to form slabs. This process results in a solid and uniform surface with consistent color and pattern. On the other hand, Casting Molding involves pouring a mixture of quartz and resin into molds to create custom shapes and designs. This method allows for more creativity and flexibility in countertop design. Both methods offer durable and stylish countertop options for consumers.</p></p>
