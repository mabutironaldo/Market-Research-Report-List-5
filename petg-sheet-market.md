<p><p>PETG sheets are available in three different market types based on their production method and application. Extruded Grade PETG sheets are produced through extrusion, making them ideal for applications requiring high transparency and impact resistance. Injection Molding Grade PETG sheets are specifically designed for use in injection molding processes, offering excellent dimensional stability and chemical resistance. Blow Molding Grade PETG sheets are suitable for blow molding processes, providing good processability and impact strength for applications such as packaging and containers.</p></p>
