<p><p>Fluoropolymer Heat Exchangers come in various types such as Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers, Reactor Coil Heat Exchangers, and Immersion Coil Heat Exchangers. Shell and Tube Heat Exchangers are one of the most commonly used types, consisting of tubes enclosed in a shell where one fluid passes through the tubes while the other flows over the tubes. Reactor Coil Heat Exchangers are designed for high-pressure applications, while Immersion Coil Heat Exchangers are submerged in the fluid for efficient heat transfer.</p></p>
