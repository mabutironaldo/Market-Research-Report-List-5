<p><p>The Polymer Additives Market consists of various types of additives that enhance the performance of polymers. Light stabilizers protect polymers from degradation caused by UV radiation. Processing stabilizers improve the processing characteristics of polymers during manufacturing. Antioxidants prevent polymer degradation due to oxidation. Sulfur-containing co-stabilizers work in conjunction with other stabilizers to provide enhanced protection. Metal deactivators prevent discoloration and degradation caused by metal impurities. Antistatic agents reduce the buildup of static electricity on polymer surfaces.</p></p>
