<p><p>Industrial wood chippers are categorized into different types based on their operating mechanisms and designs. Disc chippers utilize a rotating disc with sharp blades to cut wood into chips. Screw chippers use a screw-like mechanism to process the wood. Drum chippers have a cylindrical drum with blades that shred the wood. Other types of wood chippers include portable and stationary models. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications in the forestry and wood processing industries.</p></p>
