<p><p>Analog time switches can be categorized into two main types based on their installation method: plug-in analog time switches and in-wall analog time switches. Plug-in analog time switches are convenient and easy to install, as they simply plug into an electrical outlet. On the other hand, in-wall analog time switches are designed to be hardwired into the electrical system of a building, providing a more permanent and integrated solution. Both types of analog time switches serve the same function of automatically controlling the activation and deactivation of electrical devices based on pre-set schedules.</p></p>
