<p><p>Semi-automatic bagging machines come in two main types: horizontal bagging machines and vertical bagging machines. Horizontal bagging machines are ideal for packaging products that are longer in length or irregularly shaped, as they lay the product flat before sealing it in a bag. Vertical bagging machines, on the other hand, are best suited for packaging products that are taller in height and uniform in shape, as they stand the product upright before sealing it in a bag.</p></p>
