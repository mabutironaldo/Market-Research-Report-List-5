<p><strong>The Diode-Pumped Lasers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Pump Laser</li><li>Quasi-Continuous Pump Laser</li><li>CW-Pumped Laser</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diode-Pumped Lasers come in various types based on the pumping method used. Pulsed Pump Lasers are ideal for applications requiring high peak power. Quasi-Continuous Pump Lasers provide a balance between pulse and continuous wave operation. CW-Pumped Lasers are suitable for continuous wave output. Other types of diode-pumped lasers include mode-locked lasers, amplified spontaneous emission lasers, and Q-switched lasers, each offering unique capabilities for specific applications in industries such as medical, defense, and communications.</p></p>
