<p><p>Automatic control valves market offers various types such as angle seat piston valve, globe valve, butterfly valve, ball valve, pinch valve, and diaphragm valves. Angle seat piston valve provides accurate control of fluid flow, while globe valve is suitable for controlling high flow rates. Butterfly valve is known for its low pressure drop, while ball valve offers tight shut-off capabilities. Pinch valve is ideal for handling abrasive materials, and diaphragm valves are reliable in controlling corrosive fluids. Each valve type caters to different applications based on their unique features and benefits.</p></p>
