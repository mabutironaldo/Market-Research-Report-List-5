<p><p>The automotive gear market is segmented into various types, including spur gear, bevel gear, screw gear, miter gear, and other types. Spur gears are the most common type, characterized by straight teeth on parallel shafts. Bevel gears have conical-shaped teeth for intersecting shafts. Screw gears have helical teeth for non-parallel shafts. Miter gears are used for right angle drives. Other types include worm gears, rack and pinion gears, and planetary gears, each serving specific purposes in automotive applications.</p></p>
