<p><strong>The Flight Vehicle Propulsion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Reaction Propulsion System</li><li>Indirect Reaction Propulsion System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Reaction Propulsion Systems involve the direct ejection of mass to generate thrust, such as in a rocket engine or a jet engine. Indirect Reaction Propulsion Systems involve the creation of thrust by accelerating a working fluid, such as air or water, which is then expelled to produce thrust, as seen in propeller-driven aircraft or gas turbine engines. Both types of propulsion systems play a crucial role in the global flight vehicle propulsion systems market by powering various types of aircraft for different purposes.</p></p>
