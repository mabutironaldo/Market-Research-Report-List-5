<p><p>Scanning Probe Microscopes (SPM) are advanced tools used for imaging and analyzing materials at the nanoscale. Three main types of SPM are Atomic Force Microscopes (AFM), Laser Force Microscopes, and Magnetic Force Microscopes. AFM uses a sharp tip to scan the surface of a sample and measure forces between the tip and the sample. Laser Force Microscopes use laser beams to detect forces between the tip and the sample. Magnetic Force Microscopes detect magnetic forces between the tip and the sample. These SPM types are crucial for research in various fields such as nanotechnology and material science.</p></p>
