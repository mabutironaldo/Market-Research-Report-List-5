<p><p>Infusion consumables market types include resin inflow tubing, spiral wrap, T pieces, flow mesh, and others. Resin inflow tubing is used to deliver resin into the infusion process, while spiral wrap is used to protect and organize tubing. T pieces enable branching of tubing for multiple connections, and flow mesh aids in controlling the flow rate. Other types of infusion consumables may include items like connectors, clamps, and filters that play integral roles in the infusion process.</p></p>
