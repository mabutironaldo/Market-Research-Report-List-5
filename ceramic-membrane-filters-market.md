<p><p>Ceramic membrane filters are widely used in various industries for filtration processes. Flat-sheet membrane filters are thin, flat membranes that are stacked together to provide a large filtration area. Pipe membrane filters, on the other hand, are cylindrical in shape and are used for applications where a compact design is required. Both types of filters offer high efficiency in separating solids from liquids, making them essential components in wastewater treatment, food and beverage processing, and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
