<p><ul><li>Non-Porous Cordierite</li><li>Porous Cordierite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Synthetic Cordierite is commonly found in two main types: Non-Porous Cordierite and Porous Cordierite. Non-Porous Cordierite is dense and impermeable, making it ideal for applications where high mechanical strength and thermal shock resistance are required. On the other hand, Porous Cordierite has a network of interconnected pores, providing enhanced thermal insulation and filtration capabilities. Porous Cordierite is commonly used in catalytic converters, diesel particulate filters, and other applications where high porosity is advantageous. Both types of Cordierite offer unique properties and find diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
