<p><p>Organic pigment dispersions are used in various industries such as paints, coatings, inks, and plastics. Two main types of organic pigment dispersions available in the market are water-based and oil-based. Water-based pigment dispersions are environmentally friendly, offer easy cleanup, and are commonly used in industries where low VOC emissions are crucial. On the other hand, oil-based pigment dispersions provide better adhesion, resistance to chemicals, and durability, making them suitable for applications requiring long-lasting color retention.</p></p>
