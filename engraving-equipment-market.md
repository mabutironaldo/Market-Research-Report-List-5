<p><p>Engraving equipment market is divided into two main types: Laser Engraving and Mechanical Engraving. Laser engraving uses high-powered lasers to etch designs onto various materials with high precision and speed. Mechanical engraving, on the other hand, utilizes physical tools to carve designs into materials. Laser engraving is known for its versatility, while mechanical engraving is preferred for its durability and ability to work on a wider range of materials. Both types cater to different customer needs and applications in the engraving industry.</p></p>
