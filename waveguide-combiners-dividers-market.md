<p><p>Waveguide combiners and dividers are devices used in microwave and RF communication systems to combine or divide electromagnetic signals in waveguide systems. Waveguide combiners are used to combine multiple input signals into a single output signal, while dividers are used to split a single input signal into multiple output signals. These devices are essential components in various applications including radar systems, satellite communication, and wireless communication networks.</p><p>The waveguide combiners dividers market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.40% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing demand for high-performance RF communication systems in various industries such as aerospace, defense, and telecommunications.
