<p><strong>The SSL Certification Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Organization Validation (OV)</li><li>Domain Validation (DV)</li><li>Extended Validation (EV)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SSL Certification Market includes three main types: Organization Validation (OV), Domain Validation (DV), and Extended Validation (EV). OV certificates validate the organization behind the domain, ensuring a higher level of trust and security. DV certificates check the ownership of the domain, providing basic encryption. EV certificates offer the highest level of validation by verifying the legal entity behind the website, displaying a green address bar in browsers for added trust. Each type caters to different security needs and levels of assurance.</p></p>
