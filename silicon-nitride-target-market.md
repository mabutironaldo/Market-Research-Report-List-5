<p><p>Silicon nitride targets are utilized in two primary market types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are typically used in sputtering processes for flat panel displays and photovoltaic panels. They provide a uniform coating across a large surface area. Rotating targets, on the other hand, are commonly used in magnetron sputtering for thin film deposition on substrates. Their spinning motion allows for a more even distribution of coating material, making them ideal for applications requiring high precision and uniformity.</p></p>
