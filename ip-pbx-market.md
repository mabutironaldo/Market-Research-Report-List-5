<p><p>IP PBX market consists of various types of products and services including SIP Phones, VoIP Phones, IP PBX Servers, and VoIP Gateway. SIP Phones are devices that use the Session Initiation Protocol to make voice calls over the internet. VoIP Phones are similar but use Voice over Internet Protocol technology. IP PBX Servers are software-based systems that manage phone calls within an organization. VoIP Gateways are hardware devices that convert between traditional analog or digital phone lines and VoIP connections. These products and services cater to the growing demand for efficient and cost-effective communication solutions.</p></p>
