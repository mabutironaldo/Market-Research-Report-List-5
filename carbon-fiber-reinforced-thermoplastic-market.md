<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic (CFRTP) can be categorized into two main types based on the precursor material used for carbon fiber production: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP is derived from synthetic polymers and is known for its high strength-to-weight ratio and excellent mechanical properties. On the other hand, Pitch-based CFRTP is produced from petroleum-derived pitch and offers superior thermal conductivity and resistance to corrosion. Both types of CFRTP have unique characteristics and are used in various high-performance applications.</p></p>
