<p><strong>The Flexographic Printing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexographic printing machines can be categorized into two main types: unit-type machines and central impression machines. Unit-type machines consist of individual printing units where each unit is responsible for printing a specific color or part of the design. On the other hand, central impression machines have a single central drum that serves as the impression cylinder for all the printing units, resulting in higher printing quality and registration accuracy. Both types of machines have their own unique advantages and are utilized in various printing applications.</p></p>
