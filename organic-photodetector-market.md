<p><ul><li>Photon Based Organic Photodetector</li><li>Thermal Organic Photodetector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic photodetector market consists of two main types: photon-based and thermal. Photon-based organic photodetectors utilize organic materials to convert light into electrical signals, offering high sensitivity and efficiency for applications like imaging and sensing. On the other hand, thermal organic photodetectors detect infrared radiation by measuring changes in temperature caused by absorbed photons. They are commonly used in night vision devices and thermal imaging cameras. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as healthcare, security, and automotive.</p></p>
