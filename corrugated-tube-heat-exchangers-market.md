<p><p>Corrugated tube heat exchangers are divided into two main types: single tube type and multi tube type. Single tube type heat exchangers consist of a single corrugated tube through which the heat transfer occurs. On the other hand, multi tube type heat exchangers have multiple corrugated tubes arranged in a specific pattern to enhance heat transfer efficiency. Both types are commonly used in various industries for their compact design and high efficiency in transferring heat between two fluids.</p></p>
