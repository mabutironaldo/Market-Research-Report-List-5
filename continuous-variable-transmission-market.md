<p><strong>The Continuous Variable Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Belt Connection</li><li>Chain Connection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Variable Transmission (CVT) technology in the automotive industry is primarily divided into two types: Belt Connection and Chain Connection. Belt Connection CVTs utilize a flexible belt to transfer power from the engine to the wheels, offering smoother acceleration and improved fuel efficiency. On the other hand, Chain Connection CVTs use a metal chain to transmit power, providing better durability and strength compared to belts. Both market types offer their own set of advantages and are commonly used in various vehicles for their unique benefits.</p></p>
