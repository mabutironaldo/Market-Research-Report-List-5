<p><p>Single Surface Optical Flats are categorized based on their accuracy levels, including λ/4, λ/10, and λ/20. Optical flats with an accuracy of λ/4 provide a surface flatness within a quarter wavelength of light, making them suitable for general optical testing applications. Flats with an accuracy of λ/10 offer higher precision, suitable for more demanding requirements. Those with an accuracy of λ/20 are the most accurate, providing exceptional flatness for critical measurements. Additionally, there are other types of optical flats available to cater to specific market needs.</p></p>
