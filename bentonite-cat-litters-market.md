<p><p>Bentonite cat litters come in two main types: sodium bentonite and calcium bentonite. Sodium bentonite cat litters are clumping litters that absorb urine and solid waste to form easy-to-clean clumps. On the other hand, calcium bentonite cat litters are non-clumping litters that offer superior odor control and moisture absorption. Both types of bentonite cat litters are popular choices among cat owners for their effectiveness in controlling odors and providing a clean and hygienic environment for their pets.</p></p>
