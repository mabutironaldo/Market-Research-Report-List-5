<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The In-Mould Labels (IML) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Injection Molding</li><li>Extrusion- Blow Molding</li><li>Thermoforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Mould Labels (IML) are widely used in three main market types: Injection Molding, Extrusion-Blow Molding, and Thermoforming. In Injection Molding, IMLs are inserted into the mold before the plastic is injected, creating a seamless and durable label. Extrusion-Blow Molding involves placing the IML film inside the extrusion die, resulting in a label that is integrated with the final product. Thermoforming uses IMLs that are pre-printed and inserted into the mold before the plastic sheet is heated and formed, ensuring a high-quality label finish.</p></p>
