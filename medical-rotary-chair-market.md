<p><p>A medical rotary chair is a specialized medical equipment used in diagnostic testing for vestibular disorders. It is designed to rotate a patient in a controlled manner to assess balance, inner ear function, and treat vestibular issues such as dizziness and vertigo. This equipment is commonly used in otolaryngology clinics, audiology centers, and neurology departments.</p><p>The global medical rotary chair market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.90% during the forecast period. The key factors driving the growth of this market include the increasing prevalence of vestibular disorders, growing awareness about the importance of early diagnosis and treatment, and advancements in technology leading to improved accuracy in diagnostic testing.</p><p>Moreover, the rising geriatric population, who are more prone to vestibular issues, is also contributing to the market growth. Additionally, the demand for non-invasive diagnostic tools and the increasing healthcare expenditure in developing countries are further propelling the expansion of the medical rotary chair market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the medical rotary chair market include the integration of virtual reality technology for enhanced patient experience, the development of portable and compact rotary chairs for ambulatory testing, and the incorporation of wireless connectivity for seamless data transfer and analysis. Overall, the medical rotary chair market is projected to witness significant growth in the coming years.</p></p>
