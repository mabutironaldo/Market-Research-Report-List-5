<p><p>Composite tubes come in various types based on their shape and construction. Round tubes are cylindrical in shape and commonly used for structural applications. Profile tubes have unique shapes such as oval or rectangular, providing specific design possibilities. Conical tubes taper in diameter along their length, offering versatility in applications requiring varying dimensions. Pre-preg tubes are pre-impregnated with resin before curing, ensuring a consistent and high-quality finish. Each type of composite tube caters to different requirements in industries like aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
