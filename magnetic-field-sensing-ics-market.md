<p><p>Magnetic-field sensing ICs are used in various industries for detecting and measuring magnetic fields. There are four main types of magnetic-field sensing ICs: Hall Effect Sensing ICs, TMR Sensing ICs, AMR Sensing ICs, and GMR Sensing ICs. Each type utilizes a different technology to sense magnetic fields, with Hall Effect ICs using the Hall Effect, TMR ICs using Tunneling Magnetoresistance, AMR ICs using Anisotropic Magnetoresistance, and GMR ICs using Giant Magnetoresistance. These sensors play a crucial role in applications such as automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation.</p></p>
