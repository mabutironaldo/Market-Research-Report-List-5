<p><p>LGP sensors can be categorized into various types such as Molecular Property Spectrometer Sensors, Non-Dispersive Infrared (NDIR) Sensors, Catalytic Gas Sensors, and Others. Molecular Property Spectrometer Sensors detect gases based on their unique molecular properties. NDIR Sensors measure gas concentration by analyzing infrared light absorption. Catalytic Gas Sensors utilize a catalytic reaction to detect gases. Other types of LGP sensors may include electrochemical sensors or photoionization detectors, each designed for specific gas detection applications.</p></p>
