<p><ul><li>Fully Automatic Milking Machine</li><li>Semi-Automatic Milking Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fully automatic milking machines are designed to handle the entire milking process from start to finish without any manual intervention. They usually include automated teat cleaning, attaching, milking, and detachment of the clusters. Semi-automatic milking machines, on the other hand, require some manual intervention such as teat preparation and cluster attachment, while the milking process itself is automated. Both types of machines offer efficiency, convenience, and consistency in milking operations, helping dairy farmers save time and labor costs.</p></p>
