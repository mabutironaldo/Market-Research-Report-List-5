<p><p>Electrophysiology mapping and ablation devices market include conventional, advanced, ultrasound, cryoablation, and microwave types. Conventional devices are traditional methods for mapping and ablating abnormal heart tissues. Advanced devices utilize cutting-edge technology for more precise and efficient procedures. Ultrasound devices use sound waves for real-time imaging during ablation. Cryoablation devices freeze tissues to destroy abnormal electrical pathways. Microwave devices use electromagnetic waves to heat and ablate targeted tissues. These different types cater to various patient needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
