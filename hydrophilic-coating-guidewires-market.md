<p><p>Hydrophilic coating guidewires are segmented into two main types - straight guidewires and elbow guidewires. Straight guidewires are designed with a rigid and straight shaft for precise navigation through blood vessels or other narrow pathways. Elbow guidewires, on the other hand, have a flexible and curved tip that allows for easier navigation through complex anatomical structures. Both types of guidewires are coated with a hydrophilic material that reduces friction and helps facilitate smooth insertion and maneuverability during medical procedures.</p></p>
