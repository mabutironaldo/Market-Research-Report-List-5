Lead frames are used to connect semiconductor devices in IC packages. Package substrates provide a platform for mounting and interconnecting IC chips. Ceramic packaging materials offer high thermal conductivity and electrical insulation. Bonding wires are used to connect semiconductor devices to lead frames. Die bonding materials are used to attach semiconductor chips to substrates during packaging.</p></p>
