<p><p>Desktop tonometers are devices used to measure intraocular pressure in the eye. There are two main types in the market - contact type and non-contact type. Contact tonometers require direct contact with the eye to measure pressure, while non-contact tonometers can measure pressure without touching the eye. Contact tonometers are more commonly used in clinical settings for precise measurements, while non-contact tonometers are often preferred for their ease of use and comfort for patients. Both types have their own advantages and are used based on the specific needs of the user.</p></p>
