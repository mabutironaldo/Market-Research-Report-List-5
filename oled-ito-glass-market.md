<p><p>OLED ITO Glass Market includes various types based on the sheet resistance of the Indium Tin Oxide (ITO) coating. Types such as 8-11 ohms/sq, 18-20 ohms/sq, and 90-100 ohms/sq cater to different requirements in terms of conductivity and transparency. The 8-11 ohms/sq type is suitable for high-resolution displays, while the 18-20 ohms/sq type is commonly used in touch screens. The 90-100 ohms/sq type offers lower conductivity and is often used in lighting applications. Other types in the market may have sheet resistances outside of these ranges.</p></p>
