<p><ul><li>Volumetric Examination</li><li>Surface Examination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiographic Testing Equipment Market includes two main types: Volumetric Examination and Surface Examination. Volumetric Examination involves the use of X-rays to create a three-dimensional image of the internal structure of a material or component, allowing for thorough inspection of defects such as cracks or voids. Surface Examination, on the other hand, focuses on detecting surface defects or irregularities on the exterior of a material using X-ray technology. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and quality of various industrial products.</p></p>
