<p><p>Bridge expansion joints are used to allow movement between sections of bridges due to temperature changes, traffic loading, and settlement. There are mainly two types of bridge expansion joints: open joints and closed joints. Open joints are designed to allow for movement in all directions and are commonly used in moderate traffic areas, while closed joints are more rigid and only allow movement in one direction. Both types of joints are crucial for the longevity and safety of bridge structures.</p></p>
