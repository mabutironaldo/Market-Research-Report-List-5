<p><p>Stretch wrapping equipment market can be divided into two main types: Fully automatic and Semi automatic. Fully automatic stretch wrapping machines are designed to wrap pallets without the need for human intervention. They are equipped with sensors and advanced control systems to handle the entire wrapping process efficiently. On the other hand, Semi automatic stretch wrapping machines require some level of human input, such as loading the pallet onto the machine and initiating the wrapping process. Both types of equipment are widely used in industries for securing and protecting palletized goods during transportation and storage.</p></p>
