<p><p>An Internal Floating Roof (IFR) is a floating roof inside a storage tank that rises and falls with the liquid level. The primary purpose of an IFR is to reduce the evaporative emissions from volatile liquids stored in the tank by eliminating the air space above the liquid surface. This helps in reducing the emission of harmful gases into the environment, making it a more environmentally friendly option compared to traditional storage tanks.</p><p>The Internal Floating Roof Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing focus on environmental regulations and the need for emission control in various industries such as oil & gas, petrochemicals, and chemical processing.
