<p><p>Label dispensers come in two main types, manual and automatic. Manual label dispensers require the user to manually peel off and apply the label, making them more suitable for low-volume applications. Automatic label dispensers, on the other hand, are designed to automatically dispense and apply labels, making them ideal for high-volume labeling tasks. Automatic label dispensers are often faster and more efficient, but they also tend to be more expensive than manual label dispensers. Both types of label dispensers play a crucial role in packaging and labeling operations across various industries.</p></p>
