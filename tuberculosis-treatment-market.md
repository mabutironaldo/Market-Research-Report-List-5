<p><p>The Tuberculosis Treatment Market can be broadly categorized into two types: Active TB and Latent TB. Active TB refers to cases where the individual displays symptoms of the disease and requires immediate medical attention and treatment. On the other hand, Latent TB refers to cases where the individual carries the TB bacteria but does not exhibit any symptoms. Latent TB patients are often treated with preventive therapy to prevent the development of active TB in the future. Both types of TB require specialized medications and monitoring to effectively manage the disease.</p></p>
