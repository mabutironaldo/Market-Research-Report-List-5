<p><ul><li>Solid-shaft Gear Motor</li><li>Hollow-shaft Gear Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single-phase gear motors come in two main types: solid-shaft gear motors and hollow-shaft gear motors. Solid-shaft gear motors have a fixed shaft that extends from the motor casing, making it suitable for applications where the load can be directly coupled to the motor. On the other hand, hollow-shaft gear motors have a hollow shaft that allows for easy coupling with the load, making them more versatile in terms of mounting options. Both types are commonly used in various industrial applications for power transmission and motion control.</p></p>
