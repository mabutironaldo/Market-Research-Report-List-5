<p><p>Drug Eluting Balloons (DEBs) are medical devices used in the treatment of peripheral arterial disease and coronary artery disease. Peripheral DEBs release anti-proliferative drugs to prevent restenosis in the arteries of the legs and arms, while coronary DEBs are used in the treatment of blockages in the heart's blood vessels. Both types of DEBs are minimally invasive alternatives to traditional balloon angioplasty and stent placement, offering targeted drug delivery to prevent re-narrowing of the arteries.</p></p>
