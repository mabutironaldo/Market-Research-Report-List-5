<p><p>MEMS Air Velocity Sensor market consists of two main types: Mechanical Air Velocity Sensor and Ultrasonic Air Velocity Sensor. Mechanical sensors use a physical mechanism to gauge air velocity, while ultrasonic sensors utilize sound waves to measure the speed of air flow. Mechanical sensors are more traditional and cost-effective, but may have limited accuracy compared to ultrasonic sensors. On the other hand, ultrasonic sensors offer higher accuracy and better reliability, but come with a higher price tag. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as aerospace, automotive, and HVAC.</p></p>
