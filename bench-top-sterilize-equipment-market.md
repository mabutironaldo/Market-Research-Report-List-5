<p><p>Bench top sterilize equipment comes in various types such as steam, plasma, hot air, and others. Hot air sterilization involves using high temperature dry heat to kill microorganisms. Other methods may include UV sterilization or chemical sterilization. Each type of equipment offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications in healthcare, laboratory, and research settings.</p></p>
