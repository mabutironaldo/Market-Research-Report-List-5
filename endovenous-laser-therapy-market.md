<p><p>Endovenous Laser Therapy (EVLT) market includes two main types of products - Endovenous Laser Systems and Endovenous Laser Fibres. Endovenous Laser Systems are medical devices used to apply laser energy to the veins, typically through a small incision in the skin, to treat varicose veins. Endovenous Laser Fibres, on the other hand, are the disposable components of the system that deliver the laser energy directly to the targeted vein. Both products play a crucial role in the effectiveness and success of EVLT procedures.</p></p>
