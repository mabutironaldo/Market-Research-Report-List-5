<p><p>Single-Phase AC Gear-Motors come in two main types - Synchronous and Asynchronous. Synchronous motors operate at a constant speed determined by the frequency of the electrical supply. They are known for their higher efficiency and power factor. On the other hand, Asynchronous motors have variable speeds and are more economical. They are popular in applications where speed control is important. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
