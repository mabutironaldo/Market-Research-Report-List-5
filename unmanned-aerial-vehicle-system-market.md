Fixed-wing UAVs have a fixed wing structure similar to traditional airplanes and are efficient for long-range flights. Rotary wing UAVs have rotating wings like helicopters, allowing for vertical take-off and landing. Flapping-wing UAVs mimic the flight of birds with flapping wings. Airship UAVs are balloon-like with the ability to hover in the air for long periods.</p></p>
