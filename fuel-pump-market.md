<p><ul><li>Common Fuel Pump</li><li>GDI Fuel Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel pump market is divided into two main types: common fuel pump and GDI fuel pump. The common fuel pump is a traditional type that is used in most vehicles and operates by delivering fuel from the tank to the engine at a constant rate. On the other hand, GDI fuel pumps are specifically designed for vehicles with gasoline direct injection systems, which require higher pressure to deliver fuel to the engine. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring proper fuel delivery and engine performance in vehicles.</p></p>
