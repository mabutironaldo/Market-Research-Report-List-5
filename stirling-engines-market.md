<p><p>Stirling Engines are classified into three main types based on their design and working principle: Alpha Stirling Engines, Beta Stirling Engines, and Gamma Stirling Engines. Alpha Stirling Engines have two separate chambers for the hot and cold gases, while Beta Stirling Engines have only one chamber that alternately expands and compresses the gas. Gamma Stirling Engines have a single chamber with a displacer that moves the gas between the hot and cold sections. Each type of Stirling Engine has its own unique applications and benefits in the market.</p></p>
