<p><p>End mills market can be categorized into three main types based on cutting edge designs: Peripheral Cutting Edge, End Cutting Edge, and Others. Peripheral Cutting Edge end mills have cutting edges along the side of the tool for cutting and shaping materials. End Cutting Edge end mills have cutting edges at the tip of the tool for plunging and drilling operations. The Others category includes various designs such as roughing end mills and ball nose end mills for specific machining applications. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities in different manufacturing processes.</p></p>
